PHISHING RED FLAGS IDENTIFIED

1. Urgent Language
The email creates fear by asking the user to act immediately.

2. Generic Greeting
The email does not mention the user's real name.

3. Suspicious Link
The link does not belong to a trusted domain.

4. Request for Credentials
The login page asks for email and password.

5. No Official Branding
There is no official logo or verified sender information.

6. Poor Verification
No proper authentication or secure verification is shown.

Conclusion:
These indicators clearly show that the email is a phishing attempt.
